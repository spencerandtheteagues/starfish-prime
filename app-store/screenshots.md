1. **0-3s**: App launch with logo animation

3. **8-12s**: Sunny responding with weather + orb animation
4. **12-17s**: Caregiver dashboard showing real-time update
5. **17-22s**: Medication reminder with voice confirmation
6. **22-27s**: Daily AI insights report being delivered
7. **27-30s**: "SilverGuard - Peace of Mind for Your Family"

### Video Specs
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080 (portrait) or device-specific
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Format: H.264, .mov or .mp4
- No device frame required

## Design Guidelines

### Colors Used
- Primary Purple: #7C3AED
- Purple Light: #A78BFA
- Success Green: #10B981
- Warning Orange: #F59E0B
- Error Red: #DC2626
- Background: #F8FAFC to #E2E8F0 gradient

### Typography
- Headlines: System Bold, 28-40pt
- Body: System Regular, 16-20pt
- All text must be readable at a glance

### Visual Effects
- Gradient overlays on all screenshots
- Soft shadows (0.15 opacity)
- Rounded corners (16-24px)
- Subtle glow effects on active elements

## Captions / Badges (Optional Overlay Text)

Use Apple's built-in screenshot caption feature or overlay text:
- Font: SF Pro Display Bold
- Color: White with subtle drop shadow
- Position: Bottom third of screenshot
- Max 10 words per caption
