# SilverGuard Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 2024**
**Last Updated: January 2024**

SilverGuard Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of seniors and their caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SilverGuard mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Senior Profile**: Name, date of birth, address, preferred nickname, timezone
- **Care Information**: Medications, appointments, health logs, contacts
- **Communication Data**: Messages between caregivers and seniors, voice conversation transcripts with Sunny AI

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
- **Location Data**: When enabled, for weather updates and geofencing safety features
- **Analytics**: Crash reports, performance data (anonymized)

### 1.3 Voice Conversations with Sunny AI
- Voice data is processed in real-time and NOT stored as audio recordings
- Conversation transcripts may be temporarily stored for context (maximum 24 hours)
- AI-generated summaries (not full transcripts) may be shared with designated caregivers based on privacy settings

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Primary Uses
- Provide personalized AI companion (Sunny) conversations
- Send medication reminders and appointment notifications
- Enable communication between seniors and caregivers
- Generate health insights and wellness reports
- Ensure safety through emergency features and geofencing

### 2.2 We DO NOT
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Store voice recordings beyond real-time processing
- Share full conversation transcripts without explicit consent

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 With Care Team Members
- Caregivers you authorize can view: medication adherence, appointments, health logs, AI-generated summaries
- Configurable privacy levels: "Full Excerpt" or "Summary Only" modes

### 3.2 With Service Providers
- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: Secure data storage and authentication
- **OpenAI**: AI conversation processing (no personally identifiable data sent)
- **Apple**: In-app purchase processing

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or to protect safety in emergency situations.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Technical Measures
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Firebase Security Rules enforce strict access controls
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### 4.2 HIPAA Compliance
While SilverGuard is designed with healthcare privacy in mind, we are not currently a HIPAA covered entity. We implement HIPAA-aligned security practices for your protection.

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account Information | Until account deletion |
| Medication Records | 7 years after last entry |
| Voice Transcripts | 24 hours (temporary) |
| AI Conversation Summaries | 30 days |
| Health Logs | 7 years |
| Analytics Data | 90 days (anonymized) |

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Export
- View all your stored data in the app
- Export your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON)
- Request a complete data export via support

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion
- Update your information at any time in Settings
- Request account deletion (removes all personal data within 30 days)
- Delete individual records (medications, appointments, etc.)

### 6.3 Privacy Controls
- Configure voice conversation privacy levels
- Choose summary-only mode for caregiver sharing
- Set quiet hours to pause notifications
- Disable location tracking

### 6.4 Opt-Out Options
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails
- Disable analytics collection in Settings
- Restrict AI conversation logging

## 7. Children's Privacy

SilverGuard is designed for adults (seniors and their caregivers). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## 8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## 9. International Users

SilverGuard is operated from the United States. If you access the app from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:
- In-app notification
- Email to registered accounts
- Updated "Last Updated" date
