## What we collect
- Caregiver account information
- Senior profile settings provided by caregiver
- Reminders, medications, appointments and care plan data
- Buddy+ only: summary logs and reports based on caregiver logging level

## Audio policy
- The assistant is not a continuous recorder.
- It responds only when activated by wake phrase, touch-to-talk, or scheduled reminders.
- Logging requires verified senior speech; visitor speech is not included in monitoring logs.

## Sharing
- Shared only with authorized caregivers, service providers needed to operate, or where required by law.
- Not sold.

## Retention
- Default retention: 90 days for logs and reports (caregiver can reduce/increase where supported).
- Memory items may expire automatically.

## Security
- Encryption in transit
- Server-side key storage only
- Principle of least privilege

## Disclaimers
SilverGuard is not a medical device and not an emergency response service.
